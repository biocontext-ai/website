# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit [https://biocontext.ai](https://biocontext.ai) (the "Site").

## Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information".

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

- **"Log files"** track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
- **"Cookies"** are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. We use only cookies that are strictly necessary for the operation of this website. For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, visit [http://www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org).

As an academic open-source project, we do not use "Web beacons", "tags", or "pixels" to track your navigation of the Site. We also do not collect "Order Information" as we do not sell any products or services.

When we talk about "Personal Information" in this Privacy Policy, we are talking about Device Information.

## How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our visitors browse and interact with the Site, and to assess the security of our infrastructure).

## Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above.

- We use **DigitalOcean** to host our website. You can read more about how DigitalOcean handles your data in their Privacy Policy: [https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- We use **Cloudflare** for content delivery and security. You can read more about Cloudflare's approach to privacy here: [https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

Finally, we may also share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

## Behavioral Advertising

As described above, we do not use your Personal Information to provide you with targeted advertisements or marketing communications. This site does not engage in behavioral advertising, and therefore opt-out links for services like Facebook, Google, or Bing are not relevant.

## Do Not Track

Please note that we do not alter our Site&apos;s data collection and use practices when we see a Do Not Track signal from your browser.

## Your Rights

I you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights, and we are committed to upholding them. If you wish to be informed what Personal Information we hold about you or if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:

- The right to access, update or to delete. You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information.
- The right of rectification. You have the right to have your information rectified if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
- The right to object. You have the right to object to our processing of your Personal Information based on our legitimate interests.
- The right of restriction. You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
- The right to data portability. You have the right to be provided with a copy of the information we have on you in a structured, machine-readable, and commonly used format.
- The right to withdraw consent. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time where BioContextAI relied on your consent to process your personal information.

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the contact information provided below.

Furthermore, please note that we are processing your information primarily to pursue our legitimate business interests as outlined above (e.g., to operate and secure the Site). Also be aware that your information will be transferred outside of Europe, including to Canada and the United States, as part of our use of global hosting and security providers.

## Data Retention

Server logs containing Device Information are retained for a limited period for security and site-monitoring purposes and are then deleted.

## Minors

The Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.
